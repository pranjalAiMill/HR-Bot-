Employee Helpdesk & HR Support Policy

1. Purpose
The purpose of this policy is to establish a structured, transparent, and reliable mechanism through which employees can seek clarification, raise concerns, and resolve issues related to HR policies, employment matters, and workplace practices.

The Employee Helpdesk ensures:
- Consistent interpretation of company policies
- Timely resolution of employee concerns
- Proper documentation and tracking of cases
- Fair and confidential handling of sensitive matters

2. Scope
This policy applies to:
- Full-time employees
- Part-time employees
- Interns and trainees
- Contract employees and consultants

3. Policy Framework
The HR Helpdesk serves as the official and centralized point of contact for all HR-related matters. Employees are encouraged to use the Helpdesk before escalating concerns informally.
The Helpdesk operates under defined Service Level Agreements (SLAs) to ensure responsiveness and accountability.

4. Coverage Areas
The Helpdesk handles queries related to:

a. Leave & Attendance
- Leave balances
- Leave approval status
- Attendance discrepancies
- Leave Without Pay clarifications

b. Payroll & Compensation
- Salary breakdown and deductions
- Bonus and incentive eligibility
- Tax and statutory deductions
- Reimbursement follow-ups

c. Employment Documentation
- Salary certificates
- Experience letters
- Employment verification
- Offer and confirmation letters

d. Performance & Workplace Matters
- Performance review process
- Appraisal timelines
- Promotion criteria
- Grievances and complaints

e. Policy Clarifications
- Work From Home guidelines
- Code of Conduct queries
- Benefits and insurance details
- Probation and confirmation rules

5. Service Standards (SLA)
- General queries: Response within 24–48 business hours
- Payroll-related matters: Within 2 working days
- Urgent or sensitive workplace matters: Same-day acknowledgment
- Formal grievances: Acknowledgment within 24 hours with structured follow-up
Resolution timelines may vary depending on complexity.

6. Escalation Process
If an issue is not resolved satisfactorily:
Level 1: HR Helpdesk Executive
Level 2: HR Business Partner
Level 3: Head – Human Resources
Level 4: Executive Management (if required)

7. Confidentiality
All employee queries are handled with strict confidentiality.
Sensitive cases such as harassment, discrimination, or misconduct are managed in accordance with the Code of Conduct and grievance handling procedures.
